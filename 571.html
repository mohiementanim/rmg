570 WHEN TO USE A PARETO CHART ● When analyzing data about the frequency of problems or causes in a process ● When there are many problems or causes and you want to focus on the most significant ● When analyzing broad causes by looking at their specific components ● When communicating with others about your data PARETO CHART PROCEDURE 1. Decide what categories you will use to group items. 2. Decide what measurement is appropriate. Common measurements are frequency, quantity, cost and time. 3. Decide what period of time the Pareto chart will cover: One work cycle? One full day? A week? 4. Collect the data, recording the category each time, or assemble data that already exists.
