711 Design for Excellence (Design for X) Design for Excellence (aka Design for X, DFX) is a systematic approach to achieve a targeted objective. X represents targeted objectives or characteristics of product or process. DFX comes under the topic of DFSS which requires a cross-functional team approach with the involvement of stakeholders. In order to stay successful in the marketplace, companies need to meet customer expectations. Continuous feedback mechanisms should inform efforts to understand and solve these demands. Typical Design for X (DFX) considerations: ● cost ● quality ● reliability ● recyclability
