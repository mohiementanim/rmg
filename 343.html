Quality of Production: The quality level of each department is measured in DHU and Percentage defective units. Higher the value of DHU, higher alteration time and higher cost incurred in quality. 9. Downtime percentage: Downtime is one of the topmost reasons for less factory efficiency. Factory analyses major down times to control and improve machine and operator utilization. Top 5 reasons for downtime (also known as non-productive time, Off-standard time) are line setting, operation sitting idle, no feeding, machine breakdown and no planning for a line.
