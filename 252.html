251 Process flow chart of IE: At present Industrial Engineering (IE) is one of the important departments for each garment or textile factory. Today’s maximum factory is run by industrial engineers, where they have to follow a process flow chart. By which they can easily control the whole garments production processes. Process Flow Chart of Industrial Engineering (IE): Negotiation with garments merchandiser ↓ Garments analysis ↓ Make P.P meeting if all the required fabrics, trimmings, and accessories are in- housed ↓ Production target ↓ Set machine layout ↓ Line setting ↓ Line balancing ↓ Continuous production meeting ↓ Collecting production data
