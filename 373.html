372 A company is considering moving its software product into the cloud. It’s calculated that the initial cost would be around $50,000, and annual maintenance and upgrade costs would be about $10,000 thereafter. But it’s also figured that it would no longer need to maintain a bank of servers to host the development and production environments (a saving of around $100,000 over five years) and would save around $5000 a year currently being offered in discounts to customers when server outages occur.
