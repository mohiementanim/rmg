Duration and Next Level: The institutionalization level can last from between two and four years, or more. A company progressed to the next level after multiple years of company-wide adoption of Six Sigma leading to Six Sigma being embedded throughout all parts of the company. Level 5: Culture Transformation At this level, the company has had sustained success with Six Sigma for a long period. Six Sigma is embedded in the company DNA. “Six Sigma is the way we do business” is truly practiced. The methodologies of Six Sigma are applied not just to Six Sigma projects and by people trained in Six Sigma, but throughout all execution processes. Six Sigma also has been applied to the extended enterprise – customers, vendors, distribution chain and supply chain – for mutual benefit. The culture is data- driven, process- and metrics-oriented and focused on financial impact.
