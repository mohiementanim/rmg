World Class OEE There is no one ideal OEE score or OEE industry standard to use as a benchmark for every company. Rather, the best OEE target for your company is one that steadily and incrementally improves! WHAT IS WORLD-CLASS OEE? OEE is the ratio of Fully Productive Time to Planned Production Time (refer to the OEE Factors page for a graphical representation). It is calculated as the product of its three constituent factors: OEE = Availability × Performance × Quality The nature of this calculation makes achieving a high OEE score quite challenging.
