(2) Calculation of feed speed Feed speed = RPM/（ST/CM）=CM/min The higher the feed speed is, the more the time of distributing control by the sewing worker will be. Therefore, sewing time will also increase correspondingly. If feed speed is more than 445CM/min, high-speed coefficient HSF should be calculated; if it is less than the minimum value, HSF=fixed value 1. (3) Calculation of high-speed coefficient HSF=（4.5-MST）2/100+1 (4) Calculation of sewing time T=（MST×HSF×GT×CM）+17+P Where, T refers to total processing time, GT refers to sewing type (N, L, M, H) of different degrees of difficulty and P refers to discontinuation accuracy (A, B, C).17 is fixed value, i.e. time spent for starting and stopping the machine. The sequence of all operations of each employee per day can be described accurately through motion codes above, thus helping enterprises to establish a standard operating method.
