Key takeaways for manufacturing and logistics The Theory of Constraints has been a popular continuous improvement strategy for nearly 40 years, and it has many similarities to Lean concepts and tools. You can apply TOC successfully in distribution/logistics or manufacturing operations to bring you fast improvements, reduced lead times, reduced inventory, improved capacity, and increased profits. Although not easy to attain, these results can turn your business around or make your business more competitive in the market.
