Application and promotion of GSD standard time in clothing enterprises .The promotion of GSD standard time in clothing enterprises can be divided into four stages, as shown in fig.2 Stage I: theoretical training. Select employees with foundation and learning ability from enterprise for the training of GSD basic knowledge. Stage II: skill training. Training contents include the analysis on garment style, garment production process, standard for time selection for employee test and measurement of GSD time requirements etc. Stage III: establish database. GSD database appropriate for the enterprise should be established according to product features and management level of each enterprise. Stage IV: database promotion and application. New styles received by enterprises should be analyzed and relevant data should be obtained from the database. Data
