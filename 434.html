How to Choose the Right Employee Training Model As you can see, there are a variety of training models, and some will be better fits than others, depending on your objectives and audience. eLearning Industry recommends asking these questions: ● What’s the purpose of the training program? ● Who is the intended audience? ● Are there any constraints? It’s also important to consider whether you have the space and dedicated time. Would the audience be better served with an instructor-led classroom-type program or a self-paced, online learning module? Would group activities work better to achieve your goals, or is individual learning more appropriate?
