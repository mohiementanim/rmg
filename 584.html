Benefits of A3 problem-solving The A3 model is consistent, and it encourages mentoring and overall collaboration. Furthermore, it promotes the cross-organizational sharing of information and encourages learning and continuous improvement on every organizational level. Also, the A3 methodology encourages commitment to common goals and strengthens the levels of responsibility. Last but not least, you can use an A3 report not only for problem-solving but also for proposing improvements, reporting, coaching, and others.
