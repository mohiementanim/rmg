As you solve issues and mature in your use of TOC, you will encounter the need to question and address the self-imposed ideological constraints of your operation. There are some limitations that can be removed if you just know how to get rid of them. To remove your bottlenecks, you need to understand what to change, why to change it, what it should look like after, and how to make the changes.
