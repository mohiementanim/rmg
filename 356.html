Here are the key factors that separate the Loyalty Matrix from other alternatives: Scientifically sound but practical – While the science of the Loyalty Matrix is complex, the method by which the information is communicated and used is simple to comprehend. Multifaceted – Humans are complex beings; as such, it is counterintuitive to assume that their motivations and behaviors can be explained by a single metric. By embracing these complexities, the Loyalty Matrix provides a more robust understanding of customers. A blend of the means and the ends – By understanding the attitudes of customers (the means), we can better predict how they will behave (the ends) in both the short- and long-run.
