We will get more into how class RACI looks and functions later. Why do you need a RACI? The goal of the RACI model is to bring structure and clarity regarding the roles that stakeholders play within a project. This responsibility assignment matrix instills confidence in each responsible person because they know what they are doing— this type of stakeholder engagement is crucial, according to a 2020 study by Wellingtone. In fact, it was ranked as the highest-valued project management process. RACI provides a concrete and intentional framework to manage all relationships appropriately, from start to finish.
