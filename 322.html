For example, a coder creating a specific feature. The Accountable role:This is the person overseeing the Responsible person. For example, a team leader. The Consulted role:This is the person who can provide help and info. For example, a system architect. The Informed role:This is the person who needs to be kept up to date on progress of the task. For example, the user who requested the feature. Alternatives To RACI There are a number of alternatives to the RACI participation types: RACI ROLES SHORTEN: Roles are the following - Responsible Does the work to complete the task - Accountable Delegates work and is the last one to review the task or deliverable before it's deemed complete - Consulted Provides input based on either how it will impact their future project work or their domain of expertise on the deliverable itself - Informed Needs to be kept in the loop on project progress, rather than roped into details of every deliverable PARIS This is an early version of a responsibility assignment matrix, with the roles defined as: ● Participant ● Accountable ● Review required
