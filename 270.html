269 Layout Planning The key aim of every layout is to maximize utilization of available space in order to optimize the material flow, maintaining visibility or ease of supervision. The other benefits of a good layout may be improving on other productivity factors such as manpower, machine and lesser defects. Different production setups have different arrangements of resources depending on a number of factors such as product, process, and space availability, and of course the mindset of the management. Figure 1: Initial layout of the factory We observe a number of variations in the machine layouts having its own pros and cons. And we always starve for better layouts aiming for maximizing unidirectional material movement, optimum handling and storage of cut parts, half- finished parts and complete garment for easy retrieval.
