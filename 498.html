How the Hidden Factory works First time yield, or first pass yield, is the percentage of the time a product or service passes through a process step without any defects on the first attempt. In most organizations, this value of yield is far less than the reported final yield. Rolled throughput yield accounts for the hidden factory by not counting the rework and scrap in the calculation of yield. It represents the pure capability of the process to produce defect-free items. It is calculated by multiplying the first pass yield of each process step from the beginning to the end of the process.
