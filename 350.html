349 Operational Excellence by Shingo Model For organizations to be successful over the long term, leaders must deeply and personally understand the principles that govern their success. Furthermore, they must ensure the behaviors of every person who contributes to the business are in harmony with these principles. In short, the organizational culture they build must be grounded in correct principles. Principles of Operational Excellence The principles are categorized into four dimensions: ● D-1: Cultural enablers, ● D-2: Continu-ous process improvement, ● D-3: Enterprise alignment and ● D-4: Results – the ultimate end of all business initiatives.
