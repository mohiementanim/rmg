405 In this conflict resolution strategy, you find areas of agreement and try to smooth out the situation and circumvent tough discussions. The smoothing technique gives more consideration to one party than the other. You downplay the seriousness and behave as if the conflict never existed. This technique is helpful in the following cases: ● You don’t have time to deal with it. ● You require a temporary solution to the problem. ● The conflict is minor and involves less influential stakeholders. Advantages This conflict resolution strategy does not require much effort. You can focus on essential issues by ignoring unimportant arguments. Situations can be potentially handled simply while bringing harmony, creating goodwill, and providing enough time to find a permanent solution. Disadvantages If you fail to bring a balanced approach, one party may take advantage since you are giving them more consideration. Other parties not being accommodated may question your authority or stop reporting conflicts.
