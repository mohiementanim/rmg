Next Steps: Making Connections to Other Tools While an affinity diagram may present interesting data and useful ideas, the exercise itself should lead to further analysis. The team can now use an interrelationship diagram, for example, or even a cause-and-effect diagram to get to root causes for late medications. An affinity diagram, above all, stimulates discussion about a problem or issue, opening up possibilities for improvement or solution.
