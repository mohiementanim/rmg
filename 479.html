478 How to Create a Process Decision Program Chart? 1) First you start with a tree diagram of your process. Put your objectives on the first level, main activities on the second level, and the tasks at the 3rd level. 2) Then you brainstorm issues that could occur while performing those tasks. Process Decision Program charts (PDPC) uses brainstorming to identify issues. Step 3: Cross off issues that are unlikely to happen. Cross off the unlikely issues.
