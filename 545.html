Example of Using a FMEA in a DMAIC Project A company is putting together a new customer support department. As part of that process, it’s developed a number of processes for various customer queries. The Level 2 support team needs to complete a design FMEA for one of its processes. Preliminaries The team’s process analyst puts together a flowchart of the process. It shows the team five high-level steps in the process: 1. Get details from Level 1 support team. 2. Ask customers to run a diagnostic test and email the results. 3. Analyze results to find any issues. 4. Explain analysis to customers. 5. Fix problems if it’s within Level 2 abilities. If not, refer up to Level 3 support team. Constructing the blank FMEA The team lead uses Excel to create a spreadsheet for the FMEA.
